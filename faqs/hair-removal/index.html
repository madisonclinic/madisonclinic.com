F.A.Q.

Can grey hair be treated with laser hair removal? (or white, blonde, red hair)

Since the laser is attracted to the pigment in the hair follicle, this hair type or hair colour can still be treated with the hair removal laser, but will likely take more treatments to achieve successful removal.
